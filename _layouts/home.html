About Me

I'm an undergraduate researcher at the University of Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras campus, I'm in Computer Science major with a double concentration in math. Currently, I'm working in the Bee Lab under the supervision of Dr. Rémi Mégret developing a method to identify untagged bees in videos using semi-supervised learning and Siamese networks. I'm very interested in intelligence systems, computer vision, speech recognition, and embeddings.
